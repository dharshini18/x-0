Player also known as: Word Mastermind

Player takes turns in trying to guess the word

Description

Computer, the Chooser, thinks of a n-letter word and the player, the Guesser, tries to guess it At each turn the Guesser tries a n-letter word, and the Chooser says how close it is to the answer The number of x - letters correct in the right position. The number of 0 - letters correct but in the wrong position. The Guesser tries to guess the answer in the fewest number of turns. Note: The words do not contain repeating letters
